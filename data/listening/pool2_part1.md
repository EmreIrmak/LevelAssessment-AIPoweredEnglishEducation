Listening Part 1

You will hear people talking in eight different situations. 
Choose the best answer.

1. You hear two friends talking about a book. What 
does the boy like about it?  
(ANSWER: A)
a. It’s short. 
b. It’s long. 
c. It’s funny. 
 
2. You hear a man talking about family holidays as a 
teenager. What part did he find most enjoyable 
about them?  
(ANSWER: C)
a. the journey 
b. spending time with family 
c. watching films  
 
3. You hear two friends talking about a hotel they 
stayed in. What do they agree about it?  
(ANSWER: A)
a. The location was convenient. 
b. The staff were friendly. 
c. The room was dark.  
 
4. You hear a woman talking about a new film. How 
did she feel after seeing it?  
(ANSWER: B)
a. confused 
b. annoyed 
c. sad  
 
5. You hear a woman telling a friend about her job in 
a supermarket. What is hard for her? 
(ANSWER: B) 
a. serving fish or bread  
b. putting cans and packets on the shelves 
c. chatting with customers 
 
6. You hear a man talking about a band he saw at a 
concert. What does he criticize?  
(ANSWER: C)
a. the image of the band 
b. the quality of the music 
c. the length of the show  
 
7. You hear a sports instructor giving advice. What is 
he doing? 
(ANSWER: B)
a. giving the ways to be healthy  
b. describing a technique step by step 
c. explaining all wrong actions 
 
8. You hear a woman talking about being at 
university. What surprised her about it?  
(ANSWER: A) 
a. how quickly time passed. 
b. how much money she spent. 
c. how many friends she had.
